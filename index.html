About Me

My name is Guangyuan Liao. I got my PhD degree in Applied Mathematics. I am interested in mathematical modeling, dynamical systems, and coupled neural networks. I am currently working on model reduction techniques to understand the dynamics of coupled circadian oscillators with periodic forcing.
